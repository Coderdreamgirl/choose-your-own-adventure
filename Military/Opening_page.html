Considering the US Military?

If you choose to follow the military path, you can join immediately after graduating. There are six major service branches that form our nation's military. They are the Army, Marine Corp, Navy, Air force, Space Force and Coast Guard. By joining the military you can not only have honor and pride but a variety of benefits.
